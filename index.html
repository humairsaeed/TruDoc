TruDoc TruHealth Application

We are re-designing the healthcare delivery system in a way that impacts people's lives positively

By integrating and managing all healthcare entry points throughout the continuum of care, TruDoc’s multidisciplinary team of medical professionals and support staff were entrusted to ensure daily stratification and early intervention, leading to superior outcomes for both patients and payers. Raouf brought together talented individuals and renowned healthcare professionals with extensive experience in the field to offer exceptional services and build towards TruDoc’s global presence. Created as way to treat people located in remote places with limited access to local health facilities or medical professionals, telemedicine is increasingly becoming a need for everyone. Therefore, our CLM system was designed to offer accessible, convenient, and quality care to everyone who needs it.
